## Awards & Honors

**MANA Mark P. Styczynski Early Career Award** · 2024
: Awarded by the Metabolomics Association of North America for significant contributions to the field of computational metabolomics.

**LANL Spot Award** · 2022
: Awarded by Los Alamos National Laboratory for "initiative or creativity in addressing a critical need or difficult problem" — specifically for building the department's metabolomics capabilities from the ground up.

**LANL "Science in 3" Finalist** · 2021
: Selected as a finalist in Los Alamos National Laboratory's competition for excellence in communicating complex scientific research to non-technical stakeholders.

**SIAM Award & "Outstanding Winner"** · 2012
: Lead Developer on the University of Louisville team that won the Society of Industrial and Applied Mathematics Award and "Outstanding Winner" ranking in the Mathematical Competition in Modeling. Entry published in *Harvard College Mathematics Review*.

## Selected Early Awards

**Hypercube Award** · 2012
: University of Louisville, Dept. of Chemistry. Awarded for highest GPA and computational focus in the undergraduate chemistry program.

**Grawemeyer Scholar** · 2008–2012
: University of Louisville. Scholarship awarded to outstanding incoming students committed to undergraduate research.

**Guaranteed Entrance to Medical School (GEMS)** · 2008–2012
: University of Louisville. Selective program guaranteeing admission to the UofL School of Medicine for high-achieving Kentucky students. Includes clinical observation, research opportunities, and mentorship with medical faculty.
