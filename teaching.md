## Workshops

**Computational Metabolomics Workshops** · 2024–2026

Taught fundamentals of mass spectrometry-based metabolomics at multiple international conferences and university courses, including MANA 2024, MANA SODA, and graduate courses with Steven Barnes at the University of Alabama (2025, 2026).

The curriculum covered the entire computational workflow from raw data to final feature tables suitable for downstream analysis. Statistical approaches for analyzing feature tables and drawing meaningful biological interpretation were briefly covered. The workshops highlighted my own tools including PCPFM, a user-friendly interface for lower-level metabolomics data processing libraries such as MatchMS, Asari, and Khipu.

Materials were presented using Google Colab and remain publicly available:
- [asari_pcpfm_tutorials](https://github.com/shuzhao-li-lab/asari_pcpfm_tutorials)
- [MANA2024](https://github.com/shuzhao-li-lab/MANA2024)
